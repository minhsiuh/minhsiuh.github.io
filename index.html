About Me

Drawing upon my twenty years of experience in quantum computation and information research, I've seamlessly transitioned from academia to industry, marking a significant milestone in my career journey. This strategic move has empowered me to leverage my extensive global network of collaborators, facilitating the exchange of ideas and fostering innovative partnerships on a global scale.

At present, I hold the helm of a sizable research team comprising twenty talented individuals, spearheading our collective efforts towards pioneering breakthroughs in quantum computing. With a steadfast commitment to excellence, I am actively overseeing a dozen cutting-edge research projects, each aimed at pushing the boundaries of this exhilarating field to new heights.
