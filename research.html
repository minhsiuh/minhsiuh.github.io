Research vision

Building reliable quantum computation from theory to useful systems

My research connects quantum information theory, fault-tolerant architectures, quantum algorithms, and application-driven workflows. The goal is to close the gap between formal quantum advantage and deployable quantum systems that are reliable, scalable, and scientifically useful.
