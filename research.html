Research Topics

My research interests include general topics in quantum information and computation. At the Hon Hai (Foxconn) Quantum Computing Research Center, we are actively pursuing breakthroughs in various aspects of quantum technologies.

Quantum Information Theory

Foundational aspects of quantum information, quantum coding theory, quantum channel capacities, and quantum error correction.

Quantum Computing

Quantum algorithms, quantum complexity theory, variational quantum circuits, and quantum machine learning.

Quantum Communication

Quantum key distribution, quantum networks, quantum repeaters, and quantum internet protocols.

Quantum Error Correction

Quantum LDPC codes, fault-tolerant quantum computation, and topological quantum codes.

Quantum Machine Learning

Quantum-enhanced machine learning algorithms, tensor-train networks, and variational quantum classifiers.

Quantum Chemistry

Quantum simulation of molecular systems, quantum algorithms for chemistry, and hybrid quantization schemes.

Current Research Projects

At the Hon Hai Quantum Computing Research Center, we are currently overseeing a dozen cutting-edge research projects aimed at pushing the boundaries of quantum computing. Our research team comprises twenty talented researchers working on various aspects of quantum technologies.

Collaboration

We maintain active collaborations with leading research institutions worldwide and welcome opportunities for new partnerships. If you are interested in collaborating on quantum information and computation research, please feel free to reach out.
