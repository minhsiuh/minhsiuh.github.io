Research

Research Vision

My research program spans the full stack of quantum computing, from foundational theory to practical applications. I lead a diverse team exploring quantum error correction, quantum machine learning, quantum algorithms, and their real-world applications in simulation, communication, and metrology. Our goal is to bridge the gap between theoretical quantum advantage and practical quantum computing systems.
