2025 SciRate Quant-Ph Hot Papers

2025 Trend in Quantum Research

This article presents the 2025 quantum research dashboard in the original report layout, summarizing hot-topic structure, weighted heat, and researcher visibility from SciRate quant-ph papers with scite >= 30.

Top-Level Topic Heat

Ranked by `weighted_heat` to compare the relative prominence of major 2025 topics in the hot-paper set.

Top-Level Share

A pie view of how each top-level topic contributes to the overall weighted heat.

Topic Tree Explorer

Switch across top-level themes. Both second-level and third-level nodes are shown as cards so papers, occurrences, and heat can be compared more directly.

Circle Word Cloud

The final keyword cloud uses the `circle` layout and a packed word cloud engine to present the overall contour of hot topics at a glance.

High-Quality Keywords

A compact shortlist of representative phrases retained under the current quality rules.

Researcher Ranking

Top researchers ranked by `total_scite`. The card keeps a fixed height and scrolls internally while showing the top 100 authors.

Standalone dashboard generated from local CSV files.
